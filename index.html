Simple Recipes & Daily Rituals

Beetroot Powder Juice

Mix 1 tablespoon of beetroot powder with 1 glass of water for a refreshing and healthy juice.

  • For best results, take daily.
  • Consume before or after a meal.
  • A perfect start or end to your day.

Beetroot Powder Face Mask

Create a glowing complexion with this simple face mask recipe.

  • Mix 1 tbsp powder with 2 tbsp yogurt, honey, or aloe vera gel.
  • Apply the paste to your face.
  • Use twice a week for optimal results.
